About Artist

NCT Dream (Hangul: 엔시티 드림; RR: Ensiti Deurim) is a sub-unit the third from the South Korean boy band NCT, which is special consisting of teenagers in their teens. At first, This sub-unit has a graduation system where members are beyond the age of 20 would be out, but in 2020, this system was replaced and made NCT Dream a permanent NCT unit. This unit does debuted on August 25, 2016 with the songs "Chewing Gum" and seven members: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, Chenle and Jisung.


NCT Dream has just released an album entitled "Dream()Scape", with one of Smoothie's singles. This song on their new album like having a theory related to the previous songs. The lyrics of the song Smoothie invite listeners to leave things behind negativity in life and develop more self-confidence big. This song also describes NCT Dream's life journey in achieving their dreams. In this song, NCT Dream explore the various emotions and challenges they face in life.
